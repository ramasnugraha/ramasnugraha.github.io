User Manual

Car Repair Service

This is a quick guide for TRACtoGo car repair services. It will help you to understand how to request car repair when an accident happens with the TRAC’s cars that you’ve rented.

Getting Started

When an accident happens and the car is damaged, you can always ask TRAC for repair and fix the car. To do this, you will need to use the TRACtoGo app. Make sure it’s installed on your mobile phone; otherwise, you need to get the app from the Apple Store or Google Play first.

Check this tutorial to install the TRACtoGo app on your mobile phone.

Log In to Your Account

Navigation: Open TRACtoGo

Find the TRACtoGo icon in your phone app menu.

Overview:

If you already logged in to your account and previously rented the car via TRACtoGo, then you can directly tap the Servis icon at the bottom navigation menu. If you rented the car by booking it offline with our TRAC operation team and never used TRACtoGo before, then you need to log in to your account first.

To log in:

  1. To use the TRACtoGo, you will only need an account to log in:

  1. Press Lanjut to log in.

  2. Choose your OTP method. This will be used to receive your OTP code.

  3. Input your OTP code, and welcome to your TRACtoGo Homepage.

Open Servis Menu

Navigation: Open Servis

At the Homepage (Beranda), find the Servis menu at the navigation bar at the bottom.

Overview:

The Servis feature is only available when you’re renting a car for a minimum of 1 month. If you’re only renting for daily and the feature is not active, you need to call our Call Center for assistance.

To use Servis menu:

  1. Tap the Servis menu in the navigation bar at the bottom to open this page below.

  1. For car accidents, choose Perbaikan Umum.

  1. In the first page Perbaikan Umum, choose Mobil mengalami kecelakaan, and tap Lanjut.

Waiting for Confirmation

Overview:

Once you submit the report, our team will contact you in under 30 minutes to make confirmation about the accident report.

Waiting for the call:

  1. In the bottom sheet that appears, you can see the quick info of the ticket that you’ve created. You can tap the Lihat Tiket button to see details of your new ticket report.

Check for Repair Status

Navigation:

At the Servis home, click Tiket.

Overview:

You can always check your car repair status via the TRACtoGo app. There will be three (3) status for every car that enters the workshop for repair, such as: Diajukan, Aktif, and Selesai.

Checking the repair status:

  1. In the Servis page, tap Tiket at the bottom to see all of your tickets. You can tap Lihat detail to check the detail status of the repair.

  1. If you have more than one car waiting to get repaired, the list will be shown on this page. Tap the Car icon to get more detail of the status.

  1. In this more detailed page, you can see the repair status clearly and check it periodically. The status will automatically change following the car repair status at the workshop.

  1. When the repair is done, you will get notification and our operation team will contact you directly.

Note:

How long the repair process takes will depend on the damage that the car has. For some cases it may take 7 days or more. Please contact our Call Center to discuss further about car replacement when you need it.
