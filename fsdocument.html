Introduction

At TRAC, we always do our best to optimize user satisfaction.

However, all this time, our focus is always on the B2B users, and we are rarely aware of our B2C users. Since in recent years, our B2C users are increasing, that makes us think that we need to expand our reach to the B2C market. The research team also found that the market potential is significantly high.

However, this step raises a new challenge. Currently, the best way to reach B2C users is through a mobile app, and our mobile app is still lagging behind in innovation and having low user experience satisfaction. Therefore, despite recognizing the immense potential within the B2C market, we've struggled to capture their attention and engagement.

To address this, we've decided to take a bold step: We will merge B2B and B2C products and users onto a single superapp platform.

Purpose of Document

This Functional Specification Document (FSD) serves as the foundational guide for the comprehensive development of the innovative TRACtoGo mobile application. Its primary purpose is to translate the high-level business needs outlined in the Business Requirement Document (BRD) into detailed functional specifications, ensuring that the final product successfully delivers all the suggested requirements and business expectations. The FSD is a critical bridge between business objectives and technical implementation, offering a holistic view of the application's intended functionality and user experience.

This FSD focuses specifically on the Homepage and Car Rent features of the new TRACtoGo. Other features are detailed in the other FSD, and you can check it in the FSD repository. For these two features, Homepage and Car Rent, this document outlines essential components to guide the development process.

The FSD aims to minimize ambiguities, facilitate effective communication among stakeholders (developers, testers, business analysts, and product owners), and ensure that the TRACtoGo mobile application is developed efficiently, accurately, and in full alignment with the business requirements.

Scope of Project

The rebuild of TRACtoGo will take time and we're going step by step considering the amount of resources that we have. Therefore, we need to separate the iteration into multiple parts that are possible to accomplish. In this iteration, the scope of the development will be limited to:

  1. Merging the B2B and B2C accounts.

  2. Switching accounts from B2B and B2C.

  3. A new flow of car rent from start to end (from booking process to payment).

System Overview

TRACtoGo app now entering a new phase that will serve for both B2B and B2C users together in a one ecosystem. The app expected to be more user friendly, intuitive, and have better flows and user experience. The new app will also provide better account management and the service is not limited to the rent itself, but also for request the maintenance for the rented vehicles.

Objective

The first phase of release will introduces three major features aimed at simplifying account access, enhancing usability, and streamlining the car rental journey. These are consists:

  1. For both B2B and B2C user should be able to login and using the one TRACtoGo app. Users can access one app to manage personal or corporate rentals, selecting either ‘Login as B2B’ or ‘Login as B2C’ directly from the login screen. This integration allows seamless switching between business and individual contexts, reducing confusion and improving overall efficiency.

  2. Both of B2B and B2C user are able to switch account. The homepage now offers an intuitive account switching feature. Users can quickly transition between B2B and B2C profiles without logging out, making it easy for those who manage rentals in both contexts to access all relevant data and actions in one place.

  3. The booking flow will be simplified. The new flow connects vehicle booking directly to payment completion, ensuring users have a clear, guided path from rental selection through to successful transaction. This update reduces friction, clarifies progress, and provides timely feedback at each step of the journey.
